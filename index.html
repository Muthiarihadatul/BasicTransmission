About Us

The Basic Transmission Laboratory is a laboratory engaged in telecommunications at Telkom University. This laboratory teaches students about how the telecommunication system works, the tools that work in telecommunications The Basic Transmission Laboratory has an assistant division. They teach in electromagnetic practicum for telecommunication engineering students at Telkom University.
